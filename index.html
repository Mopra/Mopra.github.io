I call myself a multidisciplinary designer, this is because that I do not only have experience in designing user interfaces. Over the last couple of years, I have become acquainted with almost every aspect of design both digital and physical.

My primary field is User Interface Design, but I have been responsible for all the marketing material for Triudstyr.dk and Challengedenmark.dk. Here I've made designs for email marketing, Facebook ads and campaigns, Google Adwords and much much more.

I really love digital design, but over the last year, I've made designs for finisher t-shirts, finisher medals, inflatable arch, beach flags, rollups, magazines etc. This has made me realise that a lot of the rules for digital design also applies to physical design - and vice versa. That makes it quite fun and a very good exercise to go through.

Videos are the "new" thing on social media. Not only do you need to have graphics and awesome pictures with hundreds of filters when you post something. Now you need to think about videos too.

Through the marketing of both Triudstyr and Challenge Denmark, we experienced the power of videos, whether it was small 10-second clips or 3-5 minutes of good content, it just performed 10 times better as the normal content.

Through this experience, I started using After Effects and soon after I started using the application I fell in love. Not only could I use After Effects to animate awesome graphics and make good content for social media, but I also learned that I could use After Effects to demonstrate how UI and other things would work.
